Install

From your site

To install this app, login to your site and click on "Installer". Search for Jasper Erpnext Report and click on "Install"

Using Bench

Go to your bench folder and setup the new app

$ bench get-app jasper_erpnext_report https://github.com/saguas/jasper_erpnext_report.git
$ bench new-site testsite
$ bench --site testsite install-app jasper_erpnext_report

Login to your site to configure the app.
